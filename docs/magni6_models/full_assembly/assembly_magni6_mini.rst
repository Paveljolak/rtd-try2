Step-by-Step Guide
------------------

1. Take BASIC sheet metal part, SINGLE BALL ROLLER BEARING, RUBBER PAD and 2x BOLT M2, 5x5 ((8.8) Zn) ISO 7380 and bolt together - Tighten the hex key using just two fingers - do not overtighten.
